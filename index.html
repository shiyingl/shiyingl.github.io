About Me

Shiying Liu is Ph.D. student in the Department of Population and Quantitative Health Sciences at Case Western Reserve University, majoring in Epidemiology and Biostatistics. She got his undergrad from Fudan University, majoring in Biological Sciences. Then she got his master from Carnegie Mellon University with the major of Biomedical Engineering.
